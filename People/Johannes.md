**Monsieur Joanne d'Warwickshire** (John) is a specialist Seneschal/Steward that worked in the Castle of Kenilworth, in Warwickshire.

![](https://assets.forge-vtt.com/612d76c9aa81072b0cc642d5/worlds/arsmagica/actors/Johanes.jpg)

The castle was founded in the early 1120s by Geoffrey de Clinton, Lord Chamberlain and treasurer to Henry I. When King John I was excommunicated in 1208, John fell out of grace and had to leave the court. He was known to the Mercere network of Stonehenge Tribunal, and it was recruited and trained in his youth. He then met Richard (Ricardus in Latin), a former Mercere apprentice that join him in managing finances.

He is married to Mademoiselle Céline de Warwickshire, a beautiful young woman in her 20s. They have two children: Edward (2) and Edith (1).

## Quest

Joannes wants to outlive his peers and build a reputation within the Order of Hermes as a Seneschal/Steward. The Stonehenge tribunal is too political for his mundane ambitions, so he things that he can build that in Provençal with time.

He wants a Longevity Ritual as soon as possible.
