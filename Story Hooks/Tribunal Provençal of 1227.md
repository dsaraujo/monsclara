

The next Tribunal meeting will take place, by agreement, in the Domus Magna of the Flambeau, Castra Solis in summer 1227. The Praeco Dama of Merinta, a maga of exceptional age and power, commands respect from many in the Tribunal, and is known to favor female magi. The presiding Quaesitor will be [Beatrice ex Guernicus](Beatrice%20ex%20Guernicus.md). While she has fallen from the Flambeau primus Garus, she is still a member of Castra Solis (as the oficial Praeco).

One of the main topics is the excommunication of Raymon VII and the crusade from Louis VIII and the regent Queen.

Castra Solis and the catholic groups want to help the crusaders to quickly end the struggle. Ara Maxima Nova, Stella Durus, Miniata Sophia want to keep isolated. Aedes Mercurii and Ostal want to help the heretics, and [Tolosa Paragne](Tolosa%20Paragne.md) is completely split.

Cenobium will probably decide the outcome.

The last vote is to decide where the next Tribunal, in 1235 will be hold. Any covenants can offer to host, except Ceonobium, that is hosting this year. Miniata Sofia proposes to host.

At the end of the Tribunal the Presiding Quaesitor recalls any magi expelled by the Praeco. He then makes a speech giving his opinion on the Tribunal. He then calls for a vote to validate the Tribunal, which requires a two-thirds majority. If the Tribunal is generally run in an improper manner, for instance, if the Praeco blatantly acts in a partisan manner, manipulating the agenda and unjustly silencing magi, the Tribunal may not be ruled valid. If not validated, all the rulings of the Tribunal are void.
