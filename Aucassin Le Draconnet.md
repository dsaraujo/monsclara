Aucassin of Tytalus is introduced on page 117 of Faith and Flame. In my version, he was born in Nice, and grew up an orphan on the streets. He used his wits and glib tongue to survive, until he was picked up by a travelling magus from the Rhine Tribunal, who recognised his Gift . Shortly after his Gauntlet, he returned to the town of his birth, mostly just to see it again, and felt inexplicably drawn to one of the wells in the market square. He found his way into the regio, and felt that this was the perfect place for him to establish himself.

He could not resist interfering in the politics of the town, and he has established the identity of
Rene of Dragonianum, a prosperous merchant and inflauential member of the ruling establishment. He manipulates the town both by mundane means (he is a competent politician), and by the use of magic.

Because he can move around invisibly, and make people forget incidents in which they do
notice him, he has explored almost all parts of Beaucaire, including private dwellings. Thuis means
that there is littele about the town that he does not know. He has a bonus of +6 to Arelat Lore rolls
for Beaucaire, and because he lives there the Ease Factors for most questions are 6 or lower. (It is
easy to answer most questions about the town where you live.) An Ease Factor of 12 indicates
very obscure facts, and he knows about half of them.

He has no interest in the Albigensian Crusade, as long as it does not interfere too much with
his daily life. He did intervene in the recent siege of the town, ensuring that it was quickly
successful by manipulating the defenders, but he is confindent that his actions were legal under the Code of Hermes, as he was only protecting his home. (He is probably right about that, but his
optimism means that he is more sure that a Hermetic trial would go his way than is really
justifined.)

Aucassin’s main magical interest is in the Drac. He is descended from that creature, and he has
found carvings in the regio that seem to describe a way for blood descendants to become Dracs
themselves. He does not fully understand them yet, and there are deeper levels of the regio that
he has not yet fully explored. Thuere is one that he believes may link to the Realm of Magic, but
Hermetic magi do not understand that realm very well, and Aucassin is not an expert even within
the Order.

He has created a Longevity Ritual for himself, with a bonus of 6, and he is sure that this will be
enough to sustain him until he transforms himself into a creature of magic, and no longer needs
to worry about aging.
